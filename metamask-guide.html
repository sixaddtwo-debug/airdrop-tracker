MetaMask + Ink Setup

For mobile users | Total time: 5 minutes

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Use a FRESH wallet (create new). Don't use a wallet with real money for airdrops!
1

Download MetaMask (if you don't have it)

  • iPhone: App Store → Search "MetaMask"
  • Android: Play Store → Search "MetaMask"
  • Make sure it's the REAL one (10M+ downloads, by Consensys)
[ Search bar → Type "MetaMask" → Download button ]
2

Create a New Wallet (5 mins)

  1. Open MetaMask app → Tap "Create a new wallet"
  2. Set a password (this is just for the app, write it down)
  3. It will show you 12 secret words (SEED PHRASE)

🔥 CRITICAL: Write those 12 words on PAPER. Not screenshot, not photo. PAPER. If you lose these words, you lose everything forever.

  1. Tap the words in the CORRECT order to confirm
  2. Wallet created! You'll see a long address like:
    0x742d35Cc...3F4a
3

Add Ink Testnet to MetaMask

This is the most important part! Follow exactly:

  1. Tap the network name at the TOP of MetaMask (says "Ethereum Mainnet")
  2. Tap "Add network" at the bottom
  3. Tap "Add custom network" or "Custom RPC"
  4. Fill in these EXACT values:
Network Name:
Ink Sepolia

RPC URL (copy this exactly):
https://rpc-gel-sepolia.inkonchain.com

Chain ID (type number):
763373

Currency Symbol:
ETH

Block Explorer URL (optional):
https://explorer-sepolia.inkonchain.com
  1. Tap "Save"
  2. Done! You should see "Ink Sepolia" at the top now
[ Tap top bar → Add Network → Fill boxes → Save ]
4

Get FREE Testnet ETH (Faucet)

  1. Go to Ink Faucet (in your phone browser)
  2. Tap "Connect Wallet" or paste your wallet address
  3. Your wallet address: Tap MetaMask → Tap to COPY the address (0x...)
  4. Paste it in the faucet website
  5. Tap "Request" or "Get ETH"
  6. Wait 30 seconds → Check MetaMask, you should have 0.05 ETH

💡 If faucet is empty, try this backup: QuickNode Faucet

5

Do Transactions (Qualify for Airdrop)

Now the fun part! Go to Ink Portal

  1. Connect your MetaMask (tap "Connect Wallet" on the site)
  2. Find the Bridge section
  3. Bridge 0.001 ETH (testnet) → This is the MAIN task
  4. Confirm in MetaMask (it's free, testnet!)
  5. Wait for it to complete (1-2 minutes)
  6. Do a swap (trade ETH for another token)
  7. Do at least 3-5 transactions total

🏆 You're now qualified for the Ink airdrop IF they launch a token!
